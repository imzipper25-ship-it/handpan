Who We Are

Connecting Through Sound

Nestled in the heart of Lisbon, Ericeira, Cascais - Handpan.pt is more than just a place to learn; it's a sanctuary for sound. We believe in the transformative power of the handpan/pantam.

Whether you are touching the steel for the first time or are an experienced player looking to refine your technique, our expert instructors guide you with patience, passion, and a deep respect for the instrument's spiritual roots.
